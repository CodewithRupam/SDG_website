What is meant by sdg goals?


Ans:-

SDG is also known as global goals. It is type of goals that focuses on reducing global problems by 2030. The idea of sdg came from Rio+20 summit held on 2012. These goals are adopted by all countries under United Nation in 2015

How AI can help to achieve this goals?


Ans:-

AI can help to achieve this goal by finding the main reason of the problems. AI can also help by building smart content and more new technologies to find the ways to cope with the problems.
